Our Services

Why you choose 4Z for Quality

Consulting

We create a quality culture throughout your organization, not merely building a quality management system, & we guide all our clients step by step in changing their businesses from day-to-day success into a continual & sustainable success.

Licensing Support

We assist new & established pharmacies, traders & companies during license issuance & renewal to meet legal requirements, we help in defining your policy, vision & mission statements, & in developing a portfolio that satisfies your customers.

ISO Qualification

We carefully observe your existing quality management system & test it according to the international ISO Standards, to see if it is qualified for formal ISO certification, through a hassle-free process & the lowest cost.

Strategic Planning

We help all our clients, whether companies or non-profits, in carrying out professional strategic planning, that is, designing & executing a clear roadmap, where goals are set & resources are provided to reach these goals smoothly.

GAP Assessment

We offer this service to businesses who, are already certified but would like to outsource internal audit, or have documents but not sure if they are good enough for certification, or who need a full audit tool to conduct self-assessment.

People Development

We take great care of people, the main core of any change & development. Therefore, we provide special services within our consultancy packages to train your workforce according to the latest knowledge base & quality standards.
